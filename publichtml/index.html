Main Colleges

University of Hawaii at
Manoa

To get into UH Manoa, you need an ACT compostite score of at least a 21 with a completed essay. If you take the SAT, you will need a score of around 1150 with a completed essay. Your GPA should be at least a 2.8.


University of Hawaii -
West Oahu

The ACT and SAT is optional for this university, but taking them will improve your chances of getting accepted. You will need a gpa average of 3.15, if it's lower then the ACT or SAT will compensate.


University of Arizona -
Arizona

You should have an ACT score of arouond 21-28. The essay is optional. If you want to take the SAT, you will need an average score of 1182 with an optional essay. Your gpa should be a 3.3 average.


University of California - Irvine -
Irvine

You will need an average ACT score of 27 with a required essay. If you chose to take the SAT you will need an average score of 1286 with a required essay. You will need a gpa average of 3.9.


University of Nevada Las Vegas -
Las Vegas

You will need an average ACT score of 22 and an optional essay. If you chose to take the SAT your average score of 1135 and an optional essay. Your gpa should be and average of 3.3.
